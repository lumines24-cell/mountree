# 场景口播 Prompt

**检索主题：**`场景口播`。搜索结果强调：同一句话因办公桌、门厅、咖啡店、楼道等场景改变可信度；屏幕、公司信息和无关人脸应保持模糊或不出现。

## Prompt A：办公室“下班后还有安排”

```text
Vertical 9:16 desk-level selfie vlog in a realistic office; all screens are blurred and unreadable. A professional woman glances at the time, looks down at the attached Mountree shoe reference and says: "I still have plans after this meeting." She stands, takes two ordinary steps past her chair, then looks back with a relaxed half smile. Practical office lighting, quiet room tone, subtle handheld movement, no cuts.
Negative: no company logos, no readable screens, no medical or performance claims, no watermark.
```

## Prompt B：咖啡店场景口播

```text
Vertical 9:16 creator-style spoken scene in a quiet cafe by a window. Use the attached Mountree shoe reference exactly. A woman sits at a small table with her tote beside the chair, looks down at the shoes, then says: "This is my meeting-to-dinner pair today." She stands once, shows a natural full outfit step-back, and smiles. Warm window light, cafe room tone, phone camera at seated eye level, one continuous 7-second take.
Negative: no fake crowd, no logos, no long dialogue, no text or watermark.
```

**A/B：**场景只替换为门厅 / 办公室 / 咖啡店；台词、人物和鞋款不变，比较代入感。
