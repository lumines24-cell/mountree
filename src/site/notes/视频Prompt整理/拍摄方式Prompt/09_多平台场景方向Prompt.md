# 多平台场景方向视频 Prompt

> 以“一个真实生活时刻 + 一个鞋款时刻”为单位生成。每条为 5–8 秒、9:16、单镜头；先固定人物与鞋图，再替换场景。完成后可进入「[[视频Prompt整理/拍摄方式Prompt/08_英文交叉组合模块库\|08_英文交叉组合模块库]]」继续交叉组合。

## 每条末尾都加

```text
Use the attached Mountree shoe reference exactly. Preserve silhouette, toe shape, heel height, sole pattern, color and material finish. One person, one pair of shoes, one main action, one continuous shot. Natural ambient sound and motivated camera movement only.
Negative: no generated text, no readable logos, no watermark, no product drift, no warped shoes or feet, no extra limbs, no fake performance, comfort, environmental or safety claims.
```

## 01 公寓门厅｜Leaving home

```text
Vertical 9:16 creator-style phone video in a calm apartment entryway after getting dressed for work. A professional woman picks up a structured tote, glances down at her shoes, then takes one small step toward the door and pauses so the shoe profile is clear. Soft morning window light, natural room tone, subtle handheld reframe, one continuous 6-second shot.
```

## 02 电梯镜前｜Last outfit check

```text
Vertical 9:16 elevator mirror-selfie video. A woman in neutral workwear stands slightly off-center in a clean residential elevator. She adjusts the tote strap, glances at her reflection, then angles one toe forward and holds for a beat. Practical overhead lighting, quiet elevator ambience, gentle phone micro-shake, one continuous shot.
```

## 03 办公走廊｜Between meetings

```text
Vertical 9:16 low three-quarter phone video in a bright modern office corridor. A woman walks three ordinary steps past the camera, then turns one foot slightly to reveal the side profile. Practical office lighting with soft window fill, quiet footsteps, stable ankle-height framing, no crowd or visible company marks.
```

## 04 办公桌到晚餐｜Desk-to-dinner

```text
Vertical 9:16 desk-level selfie vlog in a quiet office; all screens are blurred and unreadable. A woman glances at the time, says: "I still have plans after this meeting," then stands from her chair and takes two relaxed steps. Natural office room tone, small motivated camera adjustment, one continuous 7-second take.
```

## 05 咖啡店窗边｜Work break

```text
Vertical 9:16 creator-style scene at a quiet cafe window seat with no visible brand logos. A tote sits by the chair. A woman stands once, looks down at the shoes, then steps back to show a full work outfit. Warm daylight, gentle cafe ambience, seated-eye-level phone framing, one continuous take.
```

## 06 城市人行道｜Office exterior

```text
Vertical 9:16 street-style shoe shot outside a modern office building. At ankle height, the camera tracks beside a woman in neutral workwear as she takes three relaxed steps along a clean city sidewalk. It stops while she angles one toe outward for a clear silhouette view. Soft overcast daylight, natural city ambience, no dramatic movement.
```

## 07 酒店出差｜Pack one pair

```text
Vertical 9:16 hand-held hotel-room video before a work trip. An open unbranded suitcase sits on the bed. A woman places one shoe beside the suitcase, holds the profile briefly toward the lens, then closes the suitcase. Warm practical light mixed with window light, quiet room ambience, one simple sequence.
```

## 08 衣帽间｜Choose the pair

```text
Vertical 9:16 mirror outfit-check in a minimalist wardrobe space. A woman looks down at the shoes, smooths one trouser cuff, then takes one small step backward to show the full outfit from head to shoes. Soft morning daylight, ordinary home details, subtle phone movement, candid not polished.
```

## 09 雨天到达｜Lobby arrival

```text
Vertical 9:16 scene inside a dry, clean building lobby on a rainy day. A woman enters holding a closed umbrella by her side, sets it in a stand, then takes one careful ordinary step across the dry stone floor. Cool window light and warm interior light, realistic rain visible only outside, no wet-floor or traction claim.
```

## 10 周末文化场景｜Gallery evening

```text
Vertical 9:16 refined lifestyle video in a quiet contemporary gallery lobby with no readable exhibition text. A woman in a work-to-evening outfit pauses beside a pale stone wall, turns one foot slightly toward camera, then continues walking calmly. Soft gallery light, quiet room tone, low three-quarter angle, one continuous shot.
```

## 11 初秋办公室外｜Early autumn commute

```text
Vertical 9:16 early-autumn exterior outside a modern office building. A woman in a neutral coat takes one step down from a clean pale-stone step, then pauses with both shoes fully visible. Soft overcast daylight, one distant leaf, natural fabric movement, stable camera and realistic stride.
```

## 多平台适配

| 平台 | 场景优先级 | 生成 / 剪辑要点 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 小红书 | 对镜、电梯、门厅、咖啡店 | 先做完整鞋部与穿搭关系；封面以静帧选图。 |
| B站 | 鞋类布光、白底到场景、上脚过程 | 可用“静物 → 上脚 → 场景”三个短镜头形成更完整的教程 / 花絮。 |
| TikTok | 门厅、城市步行、Desk-to-dinner | 首秒先让鞋或完整穿搭入画；测试不同开场动作以提升停留与完播。 |
| Instagram Reels | 咖啡店、画廊、酒店、对镜 | 保留干净封面静帧；用场景反差（会议 / 晚餐）引导评论或收藏。 |
| YouTube Shorts | 办公走廊、出差打包、布光过程 | 以场景切换或前后对比作为明确结构，避免一条里塞入太多动作。 |
| Reddit / 海外创作者社区 | 卧室 UGC、参考图锁定、分镜先行 | 保持鞋图、人物说明和画幅一致；短片拆镜头生成后再剪辑。 |

> TikTok 通常优先测试首秒钩子与完播，Instagram 更适合测试封面与评论 / 收藏语境。以上是内容测试方向，不是互动或转化预测。若使用演员口播，不将品牌控制的 AI 演员包装为独立消费者证言。
