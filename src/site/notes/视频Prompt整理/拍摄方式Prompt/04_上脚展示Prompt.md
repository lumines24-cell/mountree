# 上脚展示 Prompt

**检索主题：**`上脚展示`。鞋类结果集中在上脚、行走、低机位、侧面轮廓和一双鞋的前后细节；最重要的是足部与鞋型稳定。

## Prompt A：穿入 + 三步走

```text
Vertical 9:16 smartphone product demo. Use the attached Mountree shoe reference exactly. In a bright apartment hallway, a woman in [work outfit] sits on a simple bench. 0–2s: close-up as she slips one foot into the shoe. 2–4s: she presses the upper gently with two fingers and releases it. 4–6s: she stands and takes three natural steps toward camera. 6–8s: low close-up as she turns the foot once to show side profile and sole edge. Soft window light, slight handheld movement, audible footsteps.
Negative: no medical claims, no exaggerated deformation, no text, no warped feet or shoes.
```

## Prompt B：低机位城市步行

```text
Vertical 9:16 street-style shoe shot, camera at ankle height. Use the attached Mountree shoe reference exactly. A woman in a neutral work outfit walks three relaxed steps along a clean city sidewalk in late afternoon. Camera tracks beside her, pauses while she turns one foot slightly toward camera, then holds for a clear sole-edge view. Natural city ambience, soft daylight, realistic gait, no dramatic camera moves.
Negative: no crowd faces near camera, no motion blur on shoes, no warped soles, no watermark.
```

**审核：**上脚图只呈现可见动作；不要把“走很多路”“不磨脚”等未核验结果写成画面结论。

### 穿入、站起、三步走

```text
Vertical 9:16 smartphone try-on video in a bright apartment hallway. Framed from calf to floor, a woman sits on a simple bench and slips one foot into the shoe. She stands, takes exactly three relaxed steps toward the camera, then turns one foot slowly to show the side profile. Soft window light, audible ordinary footsteps, stable ankle-height framing.
```

### 城市侧跟拍

```text
Vertical 9:16 street-style shoe video. At ankle height, the phone tracks beside a woman in neutral workwear as she walks three ordinary steps on a clean city sidewalk. The camera gently follows, then stops while she angles one toe outward for a clear silhouette view. Soft late-afternoon daylight and natural city ambience; no dramatic motion or blurred shoes.
```

### 门厅鞋型展示

```text
Vertical 9:16 low three-quarter phone shot in an apartment entryway. A woman stands near the door, shifts weight once from one shoe to the other, places one heel down gently after a small forward step, then holds a natural still pose. Emphasis on exact toe shape, side profile and heel height, with quiet home ambience.
```
