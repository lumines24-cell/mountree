# 手持口播 Prompt

**检索主题：**`手持口播`。高相关做法是眼平近景、手里有物、短句、一次表情变化与轻微手持；长台词和反复挥动会增加口型/手部错误。

## Prompt A：手持鞋款近景钩子

```text
Vertical 9:16 phone front camera, eye-level hand-held talking clip. A professional woman in a softly lit apartment entryway holds the attached Mountree shoe reference close to the lens. She looks down at the shoe once, then into camera and says: "[one short hook, under eight English words]." She turns the shoe slowly once, smiles lightly, and holds the final pose. Practical room light, ambient room tone, slight handheld micro-movement, one continuous 6-second take.
Negative: no captions, no stock music, no long monologue, no extra fingers, no watermark.
```

## Prompt B：手持“一个细节”回答

```text
Vertical 9:16 close selfie talking clip. Use the attached Mountree shoe reference exactly. A woman holds the shoe at chest height, points to [one verified visible detail], and says: "Here is the detail I noticed first." She briefly brings the detail closer to camera, then returns to a relaxed eye-level framing. Natural blink and small glance off camera, soft window light, no cuts, 5 seconds.
Negative: no medical or environmental claims, no text overlay, no watermark, no distorted hands.
```

**台词结构：**短钩子 → 一个可见细节 → 停顿。将价格、认证和完整 CTA 留到后期。

### 一个细节的近景解释

```text
Vertical 9:16 eye-level, hand-held talking video in a softly lit apartment entryway. A woman holds the shoe at chest height, looks down at it once, then looks into camera and says: "Let me show you the detail first." She brings [one verified visible detail] close to the lens, lets the camera refocus naturally, and returns to a relaxed final pose. One short line only.
```

### 出门前的一双鞋

```text
Vertical 9:16 casual selfie talking clip beside a wardrobe. A professional woman lifts the shoe into soft window light and says: "This is how I finish a work outfit." She turns it to a side profile, holds still for one beat, then lowers it slightly while keeping it in frame. Natural blink rate, small genuine hand gesture, quiet room tone.
```

### 酒店出差装箱

```text
Vertical 9:16 phone-front-camera clip in a tidy hotel room. An open unbranded suitcase sits in the background. A woman holds the shoe still at chest height and says: "This is the shoe I packed." She places it next to the suitcase, then returns her gaze to camera with a subtle half-smile. Warm practical room light, slight handheld movement, one continuous take.
```
