# 运动挑战 Prompt（视觉概念）

**检索主题：**`运动挑战`。挑战类内容依赖清晰规则、一次动作、反应与可重复的镜头；但高跟鞋跑跳有真实受伤风险，不能把 AI 画面包装为安全、耐用、稳定或适合运动的证明。

## Prompt A：低风险“平衡感”视觉概念

```text
Vertical 9:16 stylized product-motion concept, clearly fictional and not a real performance test. Use the attached Mountree shoe reference exactly. In an empty studio with a non-slip floor and a visible safety rail in the background, a woman in a work outfit takes one controlled side step, pauses in a balanced pose, then turns one foot to show the shoe silhouette. Static camera at ankle-to-knee height, soft even studio light, one simple action, 5 seconds.
Negative: no running, no jumping, no unsafe behavior, no claims of safety or athletic performance, no text, no watermark, no warped feet.
```

## Prompt B：跑跳“视觉想象”而非产品测试

```text
Vertical 9:16 surreal editorial transition, not a real-world demonstration. Use the attached Mountree shoe reference exactly. A woman in a refined work outfit stands still on a clean studio floor. A brief stylized freeze-frame effect suggests energetic motion around her while her body and shoes remain stationary and accurately rendered. Camera holds a low three-quarter angle, warm neutral lighting, 5 seconds, clearly conceptual fashion content.
Negative: no actual running or jumping in heels, no impact test, no claim of comfort, stability, safety or durability, no watermark, no text.
```

## 发布边界

- 不建议真人模仿高跟鞋跑跳或进行危险挑战。
- 不把 AI 动作画面当作防滑、支撑、耐久、舒适或运动适配的证明。
- 如果需要功能证据，使用真实、受控且有测试依据的素材；这个建议要不要和「[[02 Mountree 产品与技术\|02 Mountree 产品与技术]]」及合规笔记核对一下？

```text

```
