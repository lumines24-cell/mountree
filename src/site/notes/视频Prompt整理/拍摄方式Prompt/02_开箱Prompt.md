# 开箱 Prompt

**检索主题：**`开箱`。可借鉴的形式是顶部/45°近景、撕封条、掀组织纸、单件露出、停顿反应；不要把“开箱”做成复杂特效或让模型生成包装文字。

## Prompt A：手部第一印象

```text
Vertical 9:16 hands-only creator unboxing. Use the attached Mountree packaging and shoe references exactly. On a light oak table beside a window, hands hold a sealed box. 0–2s: the box tilts slightly. 2–4s: one hand pulls the paper tab; a satisfying paper tear is audible. 4–6s: tissue paper parts and one shoe is lifted slowly into soft window light. 6–8s: close-up of [one verified visible feature], followed by a natural pause. Top-down phone close-up, warm room tone, slight micro-shake.
Negative: no music, no subtitles, no generated box text, no extra fingers, no watermark.
```

## Prompt B：礼物开箱

```text
Vertical 9:16 gift unboxing, hands only. Use the attached Mountree packaging and shoe references exactly. A ribbon is untied on a cream tablecloth; the blank gift tag remains unreadable. The tissue paper folds back, revealing one shoe. Hands lightly trace [verified visible feature] and hold the shoe still for one second in warm afternoon window light. Quiet paper sound, slow 45-degree close-up, restrained home atmosphere.
Negative: no readable card text, no invented certification marks, no watermark, no warped packaging.
```

**A/B：**只替换开场动作：摇盒子 / 手指划过纸张纹理 / 先撕封条。其余动作保持不变。

```text

The shoe is lifted slowly into window light and held still for one second. Top-down smartphone close-up, crisp paper sound and quiet room tone.
```

### 礼物拆封

```text
Vertical 9:16 gift unboxing on a cream linen surface. Two hands untie a simple ribbon, keeping the blank tag unreadable. The lid slides open, tissue paper folds back, and one shoe is lifted into warm afternoon light. The hands rotate it one quarter turn and lightly trace [one verified visible feature]. Restrained, real-home atmosphere with no music.
```

Small focus adjustment between the shoe and the hands, natural cardboard and paper sounds.
