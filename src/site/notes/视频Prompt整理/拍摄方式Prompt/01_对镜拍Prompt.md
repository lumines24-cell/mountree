# 对镜拍 Prompt

**检索主题：**`对镜拍`。高相关搜索结果集中在电梯/卧室镜前、完整鞋部、站姿转重心和侧身角度。对女鞋，画面重点是“头到鞋”与一次脚部靠近镜子的动作。

## Prompt A：通勤 OOTD

```text
Vertical 9:16 mirror-selfie perspective; the camera is the full-length mirror. Use the attached creator and Mountree shoe references exactly. A professional woman stands in a calm apartment bedroom with oak floor, off-white wall and late-morning window light. Her phone is visible in the reflection. 0–2s: she checks the silhouette and shifts her weight naturally. 2–5s: she makes one slow half-turn. 5–7s: one foot steps closer to the mirror so the shoes fill the lower third. Natural phone-held bounce, unretouched color, ordinary home light, no cuts.
Negative: no UI, no text, no beauty filter, no extra hands, no warped shoes.
```

## Prompt B：电梯对镜“出门前确认”

```text
Vertical 9:16 elevator mirror selfie, the mirror is the camera. Use the attached Mountree shoe reference exactly. A woman in a neutral work outfit stands slightly off-center in a clean residential elevator, phone visible in her right hand. She looks at her reflection, adjusts the tote on her shoulder, then angles one foot forward for a clear shoe view. Practical overhead elevator light, subtle phone micro-shake, realistic mirrored perspective, one continuous 6-second take.
Negative: no text, no brand logos, no distorted reflection, no cropped feet, no watermark.
```
