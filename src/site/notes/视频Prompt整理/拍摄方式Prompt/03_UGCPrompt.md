# UGC Prompt

**检索主题：**`UGC`。真实感来自具体生活场景、一个可见产品时刻、短口语、轻微手持和环境声，而不是“棚拍广告”光线。

## Prompt A：发现式 UGC

```text
Vertical 9:16 TikTok-style UGC, phone front camera selfie framing. A woman in her early thirties stands in a softly lit apartment entryway after work. Use the attached Mountree shoe reference exactly. She looks slightly off camera, then says: "[short natural hook]." She lifts one shoe close to the lens, presses the upper once to show [verified visible feature], then takes one relaxed step back. Slight handheld micro-shake, practical window and lamp light, ambient apartment room tone, natural skin texture, one continuous take.
Negative: no music, no logo text, no captions, no watermark, no extra fingers.
```

## Prompt B：无脸产品反应

```text
Vertical 9:16 faceless UGC first impression. Use the attached Mountree shoe reference exactly. Hands visible from elbows down hold the shoe above a warm oak table beside a coffee mug and notebook. The hands rotate it once, set it down, then point to [one verified visible detail]. Morning window light from camera-left, ambient kitchen sound, handheld close-up, realistic scale, 6 seconds.
Negative: no text, no watermark, exactly two hands with five fingers each, no product drift.
```

| 钩子测试 | 示例 |
| --- | --- |
| 通勤痛点 | `I stopped bringing backup shoes to work.` |
| 触感反应 | `Wait—feel how soft this is.` |
| 穿搭发现 | `This changed my meeting-day outfit.` |

品牌控制演员或脚本时，不应伪装为独立消费者真实证言。

### 下班回家发现式

```text
Vertical 9:16 front-camera UGC video, shot on an iPhone in a softly lit apartment entryway after work. A professional woman looks slightly off camera, brings one shoe into frame at chest height, and says: "This is my meeting-day pair." She turns the shoe once, looks down at it for a beat, then takes one relaxed step backward. Natural skin texture, room tone and small hand-held reframing.
```

### 无脸产品反应

```text
Vertical 9:16 faceless first-impression UGC. Framed from elbows down at a warm oak table, two hands pick up the shoe as if remembering a detail to show, rotate it once, then point to [one verified visible detail]. A small natural pause follows. Morning window light, ordinary apartment ambience, slightly imperfect one-handed phone framing.
```

### 车内出发前

```text
Vertical 9:16 casual UGC selfie inside a parked car before an event. A woman holds the shoe near the passenger seat, checks it quickly, then says: "I had to show you this." She brings the shoe closer for a clear profile, smiles lightly, and lowers it back into frame. Natural car interior light, quiet street ambience, slight focus hunting as a real phone camera would do.
```
