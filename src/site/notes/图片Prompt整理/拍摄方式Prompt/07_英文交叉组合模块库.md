# 英文图片 Prompt 交叉组合模块库

> 从每一列选一项，组合成新图。不要把同一列的所有选项都塞进一个 Prompt。

## 组合公式

```text
[SUBJECT] + [LOCATION / SURFACE] + [ONE PRODUCT MOMENT] + [LIGHT] + [CAMERA] + [MOOD / SPACE] + [共同约束]
```

## 场景池

- `a calm apartment bedroom with oak flooring`
- `a clean residential elevator with brushed metal walls`
- `a bright modern office corridor`
- `a quiet cafe window seat with no visible logos`
- `a warm oak table beside a window`
- `a pale travertine pedestal against a cream background`
- `a minimalist wardrobe space with soft morning daylight`
- `a clean city sidewalk outside a modern office building`

## 产品时刻池

- `one foot slightly forward for a clear shoe profile`
- `one shoe lifted into soft window light`
- `tissue paper folded back to reveal one shoe`
- `hands pointing to one verified visible detail`
- `a precise side profile and sole edge`
- `one shoe resting naturally beside a structured tote`
- `one foot slipping into the shoe on a simple bench`
- `one pair arranged with generous negative space`

## 光线池

- `late-morning window light from camera-left`
- `soft overcast daylight with natural pavement texture`
- `warm late-afternoon side light`
- `practical office overhead light with window fill`
- `large softbox from upper left and subtle reflected fill`
- `soft morning daylight with low-contrast shadows`

## 镜头池

- `50mm lens, eye-level three-quarter framing`
- `85mm lens, refined product-detail composition`
- `100mm macro lens, focus locked on one material detail`
- `slightly overhead smartphone composition`
- `ankle-height low three-quarter perspective`
- `full-length mirror-selfie perspective`

## 情绪 / 留白池

- `restrained premium lifestyle editorial, 4:5 vertical`
- `candid creator-made realism with subtle imperfection, 4:5 vertical`
- `calm professional workday mood, 4:5 vertical`
- `clean upper-left negative space for later copy, 4:5 vertical`
- `warm cream, stone and moss-green palette, 4:5 vertical`
- `refined natural editorial finish, 16:9 hero composition`

## 单变量测试

| 批次 | 固定 | 只替换 | 目的 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| A | 鞋图、人物、镜头 | 场景 | 找最贴近使用时刻的场景 |
| B | 鞋图、人物、场景 | 产品时刻 | 找鞋型最稳定的动作 / 物件关系 |
| C | 鞋图、人物、场景、动作 | 光线 | 找真实感与高级感平衡 |
| D | 鞋图和主图 | 构图与留白 | 为广告文案和封面预留空间 |

## 商品镜头组测试

| 轮次 | 固定 | 只改 | 建议生成数 | 用途 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 场景 | 同一鞋款、45° | 白底 / 橡木地板 / 石材 / 亚麻 | 4 | 找品牌底色 |
| 光线 | 同一场景 | 阴天窗光 / 晨光 / 暖灯 / 柔箱 | 4 | 找真实与高级的平衡 |
| 构图 | 同一产品、同光 | 45° / 侧面 / 俯拍 / 微距 | 4 | 建立商品页镜头组 |
| 人物 | 同一鞋款、同场景 | 对镜 / 站姿 / 坐姿 / 行走 | 4 | 找社媒穿搭适配 |
| 留白 | 同一 Hero | 左留白 / 右留白 / 上留白 | 3 | 匹配官网与广告文案 |

Instagram 适合先测 4:5 的收藏型穿搭/细节图；小红书优先测完整脚部、自然生活痕迹和对镜视角。测试结果取决于封面、文案、受众和发布节奏，不构成互动或转化预测。
