## 办公走廊上脚

```text
Low-angle full-leg photograph of a woman walking in a bright modern office corridor; one foot is mid-step but naturally grounded, shoes sharply readable, [work outfit] visible from mid-thigh down. Soft overhead practical light with window fill, 50mm lens, f/5.6, realistic gait and fabric movement, quiet professional palette, 4:5 vertical.
```

## 门厅坐姿穿入

```text
Calf-to-floor photograph in a bright apartment entryway. A woman sits on a simple bench and slips one foot into the shoe, hands naturally placed, the shoe profile and toe shape clearly visible. Soft window light, realistic oak floor, 50mm lens, natural contact shadow, 4:5 vertical.
```

## 城市路面低机位

```text
Ankle-height street-style photograph of a woman in neutral workwear taking one ordinary step on a clean city sidewalk. The shoe is crisp and proportionate, soft late-afternoon daylight, natural city background softly out of focus, 50mm lens, no motion blur on the product, 4:5 vertical.
```

## 手持鞋款细节

```text
Hands-only premium product detail image. Two elegant hands hold one shoe at chest-height against a softly lit cream wall, turning the shoe slightly to show [one verified visible detail]. Shallow depth of field, natural window light, realistic skin texture and hand anatomy, product remains in sharp focus, 4:5 vertical.
```
