# 场景封面与概念 Prompt

> 适用：社媒封面、系列主题图、活动视觉。文字、CTA、价格和认证后期添加；所有 Prompt 后加「[[图片Prompt整理/拍摄方式Prompt/00_图片Prompt索引#每条 Prompt 末尾都加\|00_图片Prompt索引#每条 Prompt 末尾都加]]」。

## 办公桌到晚餐

```text
Vertical 4:5 lifestyle cover image in a quiet office, with all screens blurred and unreadable. A professional woman sits at the edge of a desk, legs angled naturally so the shoes are visible, structured tote nearby. Practical office lighting with soft window fill, calm neutral palette, clean upper-third negative space for later copy.
```

## 咖啡店会议日

```text
Vertical 4:5 editorial lifestyle image at a quiet cafe window. A professional woman stands beside her table, one foot naturally forward, a closed notebook and tote on the chair, no visible brand logos. Warm daylight, restrained composition, clear product silhouette, upper-left negative space for later copy.
```

## 城市傍晚通勤

```text
Vertical 4:5 city lifestyle image outside a modern office building at blue hour. A woman in a neutral blazer walks calmly toward a softly lit entrance, shoes remain sharp and visible, warm interior light contrasts with cool evening tones, natural stride, understated premium editorial feel.
```

## 概念时装定格

```text
Vertical 4:5 conceptual fashion image in a clean studio with pale stone floor and warm cream background. A woman in refined workwear stands in a grounded pose; a soft fabric sweep crosses the foreground while the shoes remain still and precisely rendered. Low three-quarter camera, subtle editorial movement illusion, no performance or safety claim.
```

## 节日送礼留白

```text
Vertical 4:5 premium seasonal still life: one shoe and its open plain box on cream linen, a simple ribbon and a single evergreen sprig placed far from the product. Warm late-afternoon side light, quiet natural shadows, refined minimal gift atmosphere, generous blank upper area for later campaign copy.
```
