# 生活方式与 Hero Prompt

> 适用：Shopify、广告背景、Instagram 产品种草。所有 Prompt 后加「[[图片Prompt整理/拍摄方式Prompt/00_图片Prompt索引#每条 Prompt 末尾都加\|00_图片Prompt索引#每条 Prompt 末尾都加]]」。

## 窗边通勤静物

```text
One pair of shoes placed naturally beside a structured work tote, a closed notebook and a ceramic coffee cup on a warm oak floor near a window. Late-morning window light from camera-left, soft real shadows, one small lived-in detail such as a folded blazer sleeve. Slightly above eye-level three-quarter angle, 50mm lens, f/4, restrained premium lifestyle editorial, 4:5 with clear space above for later copy.
```

## 门厅出门场景

```text
One pair of shoes placed at the edge of a calm apartment entryway beside a neutral coat and structured tote. Soft morning daylight falls across the oak floor, realistic contact shadows, slight asymmetry like a real leaving-home moment, 50mm lens, natural warm-neutral palette, 4:5 vertical.
```

## 官网 Hero 左留白

```text
A single shoe placed on a pale travertine pedestal against a softly graded warm cream background. The shoe sits in the lower-right third, leaving the left half clean negative space for post-production copy. Large softbox from upper left, reflected fill from right, subtle grounded shadow, 85mm lens, f/5.6, restrained moss-green accent, 16:9.
```

## 秋冬城市石材场景

```text
One pair of shoes on a clean pale-stone step outside a modern office building in early autumn. Soft overcast daylight, a single fallen leaf in the distance, refined neutral styling, realistic exterior texture, product remains the hero, low three-quarter 50mm framing, 4:5 vertical.
```

## 旅行收纳场景

```text
One pair of shoes placed neatly beside an open unbranded suitcase in a calm hotel room. Warm practical lamp light mixed with soft window light, cream bedding and oak details, a folded blazer partly visible, natural shadows, premium but believable travel editorial, 4:5 vertical.
```
