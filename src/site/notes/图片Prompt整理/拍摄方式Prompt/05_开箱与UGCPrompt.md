# 开箱与 UGC Prompt

## 无露脸桌面开箱

```text
Top-down candid unboxing photograph on a light oak table beside a window: tissue paper partly pulled back, one shoe just revealed, hands visible from wrists down, packaging open but with no readable generated text. Warm morning window light, soft real shadows, slight paper wrinkles and authentic table texture, 4:5 vertical, creator-made rather than studio-perfect.
```

## 礼物开箱

```text
A thoughtful gift unboxing on a cream linen-covered table: shoe box partly open, tissue paper folded back, one shoe visible, a simple ribbon and a blank unmarked gift tag. Warm late-afternoon side light, muted cream, oak and moss-green palette, 85mm lens, f/2.8, calm premium but believable home atmosphere, 4:5 vertical.
```

## 门厅回家开箱

```text
Hands-only lifestyle photo in a calm apartment entryway. An open shoe box rests on a bench, tissue paper is folded back and one shoe is being lifted out with both hands. Structured tote and neutral coat softly out of focus, late-afternoon window light, authentic unboxing moment, 4:5 vertical.
```

## 创作者桌面第一印象

Natural morning window light, slight smartphone-camera imperfection, realistic scale, 4:5 vertical.
